About Me

Where Creativity
Meets Execution

I'm a graphic designer and project manager specialized in communication and advertising media. My career spans the full spectrum — from creative direction and visual design to hands-on production management including cutting, printing, and material procurement.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge the creative and operational worlds: I design with precision and manage projects with structure. I've built an extensive network of trusted collaborators — printers, installers, photographers, fabricators — that allows me to deliver ambitious projects on time and on budget.
